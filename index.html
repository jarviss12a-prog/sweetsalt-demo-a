Our Story

Where Paris Meets
Lincoln Park

Sweet&Salt was born from a simple belief: that the neighborhood deserves the same quality you'd find in a Parisian patisserie — without the plane ticket.

Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to laminate croissant dough by hand, proof breads to perfection, and craft European desserts using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

We source the finest ingredients — real butter, premium chocolate, seasonal fruits — because shortcuts have no place in our kitchen. From the first sip of espresso to the last flake of pastry, every detail matters.

— The Sweet&Salt Team
